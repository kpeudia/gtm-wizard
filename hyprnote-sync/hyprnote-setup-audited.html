Daily Use

Before a call: Open hyprnote. Click New Note. Click Record.

During the call: It transcribes locally on your device.

After the call: Stop recording. AI generates your notes.

Let participants know you're using a note-taking tool so you can focus on the conversation.

To sync to Salesforce:

Open hyprnote-sync folder. Double-click sync-meetings.command

Do this at end of day. Notes appear as Events on your Accounts.

Security & Privacy

Where is data stored?

Recordings and transcripts stay on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.

What about the AI transcription?

Runs locally on your device using on-device speech-to-text. No cloud processing.

What gets sent to Salesforce?

Only the meeting summary text. No audio files, no raw transcripts.
