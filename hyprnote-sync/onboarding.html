How to Use

Before a call: Open checks. Click New Note. Click Record.

During the call: It captures audio automatically.

After the call: Stop recording. AI generates your notes.

To sync to Salesforce:

Double-click sync-meetings.command in the hyprnote-sync folder.

Do this at the end of each day with client calls.

Questions

How does it know which account?

It matches your owned accounts first, then participant emails, then company names.

What if it can't match?

The meeting is skipped. Add participant emails in the app for better matching.

Where do notes appear?

As Events on the Account, and in the Customer Brain field.
