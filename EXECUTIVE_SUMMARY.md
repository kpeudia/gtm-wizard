GTM-Wizard is an **AI-powered conversational interface** that sits inside Slack and provides instant access to your Salesforce data. Think of it as having a highly knowledgeable sales operations analyst available 24/7 who can answer any question about your pipeline, accounts, or dealsâ€”instantly.

**In Simple Terms:**
Instead of logging into Salesforce, navigating multiple screens, building reports, and exporting data, your team simply asks questions in Slack like they would ask a colleague:
- "Who owns Intel?"

- "What's the account plan for Apple?"

GTM-Wizard understands the question, queries Salesforce, and returns a formatted answer in seconds.
