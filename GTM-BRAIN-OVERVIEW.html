GTM-Brain: Conversational Salesforce Intelligence

Instant answers to sales questions without opening Salesforce

Bottom Line: GTM-Brain eliminates 2-5 hours per week of manual Salesforce searches for each team member by providing instant, conversational access to pipeline data, account intelligence, and meeting history—directly in Slack and via web dashboard.
--- ## The Problem We Solved Before GTM-Brain, answering basic sales questions required multiple steps: 1. Log into Salesforce 2. Navigate to the correct module (Accounts, Opportunities, Reports) 3. Apply filters manually 4. Export data to Excel for analysis 5. Repeat for each question **Result:** Simple questions like "Who owns Boeing?" took 2-3 minutes. Complex questions like "What's our weighted pipeline for Q4 by product line?" took 15-30 minutes—or never got answered because it was too time-consuming. **Impact on decision-making:** Leadership couldn't get quick answers in meetings. AEs couldn't quickly research accounts before calls. Product teams couldn't easily understand pipeline by use case. --- ## How It Works ### Method 1: Slack (Primary Interface) Simply mention `@gtm-brain` in any Slack channel or DM with your question in plain English.
You: @gtm-brain who owns Intel?
GTM-Brain: Intel is owned by Julie Stefanich. They have 2 active opportunities in Stage 3 - Pilot totaling $2.3M ACV.
You: @gtm-brain what's our late stage pipeline?
GTM-Brain: Late Stage Pipeline (Stage 3+):
• Stage 4 - Proposal: 8 opps, $12.4M total, $8.7M weighted
• Stage 3 - Pilot: 15 opps, $18.2M total, $9.1M weighted
Total: 23 opportunities, $30.6M gross, $17.8M weighted
You: @gtm-brain show me all opportunities at Boeing

1. AI-Augmented Contracting - Stage 4 - Proposal - $5.2M - LOI: Dec 15
2. Compliance - Stage 2 - SQO - $800K - LOI: Mar 2025
3. Sigma - Stage 1 - Discovery - $450K - LOI: TBD
Total Pipeline: $6.45M

Reporting:
  • Excel export (sorted Stage 4 first)

Field Mappings (Critical for Accuracy)

Opportunity Fields:
  • Target_LOI_Date__c - Target sign date
  • ACV__c - Annual contract value
  • Finance_Weighted_ACV__c - Weighted ACV (used for forecasting)
  • Revenue_Type__c - Values: ARR, Booking, Project
  • Product_Line__c - Product/service offering
  • StageName - Standard Salesforce stage (Stage 0-4 format)
Account Fields:
  • Account_Plan_s__c - Structured account plans
  • Customer_Brain__c - Meeting notes and context
  • Customer_Type__c - Revenue, Pilot, LOI classification
  • Is_New_Logo__c - New vs. existing customer
  • Region__c - Geographic territory

System Constraints & Considerations

What It Does Well:
  • Answering structured questions about existing data
  • Providing instant access without Salesforce navigation
  • Consistent field formatting and data quality enforcement
  • Real-time data (no sync lag)
Current Limitations:
  • Pattern-based matching: Queries must match predefined patterns. "Show me Intel" works; "What do you know about Intel" requires "tell me about Intel"
  • No predictive analytics: Reports current state, doesn't forecast future trends
  • Einstein Activity gaps: Meeting data only available for accounts created before meetings were scheduled (backfill in progress)
  • Single-account queries: Bulk operations require multiple messages or dashboard view
Planned Enhancements:
  • Semantic query matching (unlock rigid patterns)
  • Queryable audit log (SOC 2 compliance)
  • Field history tracking (who updated ACV/LOI dates)
  • Cross-object queries (accounts + contacts + cases)
  • Batch operations (bulk reassignment, mass updates)
